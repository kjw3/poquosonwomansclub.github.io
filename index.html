About

The Poquoson Woman’s Club meets on the third Wednesday of each month, September to May at the Clubhouse on 9 Cedar Road, Poquoson, VA 23662. We are a group of women doing fun activities to improve the welfare of our community. Please come and check us out!

The mission of this organization shall be to promote through common interests, the cultural, educational, philanthropic and environmental welfare of the community.
